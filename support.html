Qazaa Prayers

What is qazaa and how does Yalla Pray! help?

Qazaa refers to prayers that were missed and need to be made up. Yalla Pray! tracks your entire prayer history from your prayer start date, making it easy to go back and log missed prayers.

Your prayer start date is calculated as: Date of Birth + Praying Age. For example, if you were born on January 1, 1995 and your Praying Age is set to 10, your prayer start date is January 1, 2005. Prayers before this date are not tracked.
